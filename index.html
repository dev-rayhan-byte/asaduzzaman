Research Interest

Research Interest

Biophysics, Biomedical Physics, & Sensor Tech Expert

  • Biophysical Modeling: Probing molecular and cellular processes via multi-scale modeling.
  • Biomedical Applications: Advancing drug delivery, pharmacokinetics, and precision therapies with modeling.
  • Medical Imaging & Diagnostics: Collaborating for better healthcare imaging and early disease detection.
  • Biomechanics: Studying tissue mechanics to inform prosthetics and orthopedics.
  • Bioinformatics & Computational Biology: Discovering therapeutic targets and biomarkers through data analysis.
  • Sensor Technologies: Precision sensors for healthcare, environmental monitoring, and industry.

My work merges these fields, deepening biological understanding, advancing medicine, and pioneering sensors. Committed to health, sustainability, and innovation.
